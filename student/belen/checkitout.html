What is That!?

You slowly get out of the tent trying to make the least noise possible.

You start walking towards where you heard Kevin's voice. It is pitch black everywhere you turn adding to the fear that is already there.

As you hear the crunch to your footsteps through the unknown you stop. You can sense you are in the same space as someone...something?

You whisper, "Kevin?" and remember your keychain has a mini flashlight. You reach to your pocket and turn it on, you point it right in front of you and see two small blue circles floating and realize it is a huge bear you are walking towards.

You feel someone grab your arm and gasp, it's Kevin.
