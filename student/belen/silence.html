Silence

Both of you stay inside not making a single noise and just staring at each other waiting to hear the bear again.

After a few minutes you whisper, "I think it's gone."

You slowly nod to agree and carefully unzip the tent and point out your flashlight. You see nothing and then see a bright yellow light bounce back to your eyes when you point it to the back of the tent.

Squinting to read it It says, "No Overnight Camping, BEWARE OF WILD ANIMALS"

You read it out loud to Kevin and both of you start laughing and begin to pack up.
