Arrive at Lake Eerie

“FINALLY! We’re here!” you tell your buddy Kevin after the 6-hour car ride from the city to Lake Eerie.

Kevin replies, “C’mon but wasn’t it worth it?” as you both exit the car and admire the clear blue sky and the chirping birds.

After all those hours studying in the library’s basements for so many hours not seeing the light of day you take a deep breath and ask Kevin, “Hold on, Where are the restrooms?”

He looks over at you and smirks, “You’re looking at it!” Pointing to a shrub of bushes a couple of feet away.

You sigh and say “I NEED TO GO” and start walking over.

Kevin yells, “Wait! Help me unload the car!”
