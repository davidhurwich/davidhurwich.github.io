Ran Too fast

Being an 8-month little puppy in this big world things can be very scary.

Your name is Casey and you love going on walks with your mom and your owner Carla. This time you guys went on a walk in a big green forest.

Seeing a squirrel out of the corner of your eye without hesitation you run after it. By the time you catch up to it, you realize you have run off too far from mom and Carla. Will you be able to make it back home?
