Crashing Grey

“Aw geez you scared me” you begin to laugh awkwardly, “Im just trying to find a spot to use the restroom”. He raises an eyebrow, and asks you if you saw the crash nearby. You fain ignorance, and say you haven’t seen anything that whole night, as you just woke up. You begin to ask him why he is in the forest, and he responds, “Im here on vacation with a friend. Be careful here. People tend to get themselves lost in the forest. Have a goodnight.” He turns around a disappears as quickly as he made himself known.
