Kurger Bing

You have arrived at Kurger Bing, a planet with a moon that orbits absurdly close to it and often serves as a huge travel and employment destination for citizens. You land near Bojangles, one of the largest cities of Kurger Bing, where skyscrapers pierce the clouds and aircrafts hover about as commuters rush to work.

Wallace's map tells you to go to the top of the tallest building, and an hour later with a lot of smooth-talking, you make your way to the highest floor. He seems to be the CEO of the company that is using the building, and you are shocked to see him dancing away as he spins and breakdances on his massive desk to Kurgerian pop music. Reading the nametag on his desk, you find out he goes by Gyrating Gerald.

You introduce yourself and your purpose for visiting, and Gyrating Gerald is in disbelief that you interrupted his fun. He then says he knew Wild Wallace would take things too far for his own good, and gives you a riddle: "He has married many women but has never been married. Who is he?" Depending on your answer to this, he will have his security tell you which wormhole to take.
