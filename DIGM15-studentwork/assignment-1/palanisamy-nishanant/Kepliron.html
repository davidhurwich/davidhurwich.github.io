Kepliron

You arrive at Kepliron, a planet on a different solar system. It is notably technologically advanced (even for this time period) with a high overall quality of life for all of its citizens and natural macroorganisms.

You are taken in by Kepliron's planet space security and are questioned by a very strict customs department. The stern officer whose nametag says "Larj Feller" asks you what is your purpose for arriving at Kepliron's space. You scan his impressive physique and notice he has two bulging biceps on each arm, and realize you should be honest with him.

Your answer to his question is:

"I mean no harm, I just lost my way and am passing by."


"I would like to visit your planet as a tourist for a few days please, Mr. Feller."
