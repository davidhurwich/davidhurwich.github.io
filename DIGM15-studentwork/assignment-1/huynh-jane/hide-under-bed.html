FOREST ESCAPE

Seeing that the bed has a narrow space that you can barely fit inside, you decide to crawl underneath the bed. Just as you reached the dark end underneath the bed against the wall, the door to the room creaks open. You hold your breath in fear. The shuffling in the room gets closer to the bed, and you slowly run out of breath. You try to silently take a quick breath, but dust gets stuck in your throat and you cough. The room becomes silent.
