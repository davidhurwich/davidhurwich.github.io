FAQ

What is this exactly?

Curse of the Mummy is a special trick-or-treat experience. It's a local DIY halloween experience meant for children ages 5 - 12. Kids can enter into the spooky Egyptian tomb to retrieve candy. The experience will include sound, video and other elements.

Where and When is it Occuring?

It is occuring at 1735 39th ave, San Francisco, CA 94122.
October 31st, 2022 Halloween night from 5pm - 10pm

Is it free?

Yup!

Is there Candy?

Yup!

Is this Too Scary for My Kids?

I created this experience with young children in mind. It's more spooky / creepy than scary. No blood or gore, no one popping out to scare anyone. Think Addams Family / The Munsters not Texas Chainsaw Massacre / Saw.

If children do not wish to go into the tomb they can request not to go and will be given the candy in the front of the house.

How Long is the Experience?

Not very long, I would say realistically kids would spend 5 - 10 minutes.

At the end of the day this is a DIY experience made by a passionate designer as a fun side project. But it is no means meant to be a professional experience like Disneyland. Please keep expectations at a reasonable level.

Is there anything to do besides enter the Tomb?

Yes! There will be other activites including an Egyptian ring toss and fun 3D optical posters for kids to view.

Are you Participating in the Great Hauntway?

Yes! We will be there, although the experience will be scaled down as the Great Hauntway does not want large structures.
