You walk for some time, but you notice your surroundings getting gradually darker.

It must be night.

You pull out your flashlight and keep walking for what feels like a couple of miles. By this time, it’s completely dark. You notice a glowing light ahead of you. Becoming nervous, you quickly turn off your flashlight and cautiously walk closer. You peak around the corner and see large creatures sitting around a campfire. Although they are big and most definitely not human, they seem civilized. They are talking in some foreign language, but maybe they could still help you find your way.
