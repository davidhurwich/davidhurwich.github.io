Anyways, we know about your planet you call Earth and the invasion. We call that alien race The Scavengers, because that’s exactly what they do. We think we have discovered a way to terminate the Scavengers, while saving the planet in which they are currently occupying, your Earth.

I know, the three of you must have several questions, but save them for when we go inside and meet with our council. We have some planning to do.
