Be friendly!

You laugh it off. “Yeah, I guess so.” You give your best smile. Look friendly! They might be your only chance of survival.

“What happened to your planet?” the merman asks.

“It was destroyed. I’m the last human left, so I took my spaceship and went here.”

“That is unfortunate. Despite your...appearance...we welcome you!”

Trading their fins for legs in some sort of indescribable magic, the Elleknores take you on a tour of their kingdom.

“We have plenty of good jobs here. Unfortunately, the majority of them take place underwater. Our few land jobs do pay enough—are you okay with that?”

You need the work to survive, so you don’t have much of a choice.
