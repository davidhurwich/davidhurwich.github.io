Pawians

“Hello, friend!” the one in the front says once they are close to you. They all bow, and you do the same. “How were your travels?”

“Not too bad,” you say.

“We have been awaiting your return.” My return? But I just got here. “Come now, we can get settled in. I’m sure you are tired from your long quest—you deserve rest, and a great banquet afterward for everything you have done for Whopp!”

What? Then it dawns on you. They’re blind—that explains the way they walk, their glasses, and whoever they seem to be mistaking you for.

You could tell them the truth—that you’re from Earth. Or you could play along; who knows what they think of Earth’s humans?
