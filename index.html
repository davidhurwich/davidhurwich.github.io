ABOUT

Kaboom! is an art project dedicated to the wild, amazing world of animation. As we say at Kaboom! "Live life more animated!

We show a wide variety of work including classics like Betty Boop, Popeye and Daffy Duck, to Japanese anime to featuring independent animators. We like to show all shapes, sizes and styles when it comes to animation.

Shows on Kaboom!

The Popeye Show

The super strong sailor returns to fight Bluto and win the hand of the fair Olive Oil, as long as he has his vegatable of choice nearby.

Superman

Max Fleisher's famous depiction of the Man of Steel from the 1940s. Watch him fight to protect Metropolis and save the day!

Cartoon Klablooey!

Watch a collection of classic cartoon shorts with some of the world's most famous characters including: Betty Boop, Mighty Mouse, Daffy Duck, Casper the Friendly Ghost and more!

Sci-Bots

Join the Princess of the Moon, Aurora and her three cyborg companions (Kugo, Djorgo, and Hakka) who must travel to the Great King planet and restore the Galaxy Energy in the year 2072.

Space Angel

Join the classic 1960s space adventure with Scott McCloud as he explores the universe.
