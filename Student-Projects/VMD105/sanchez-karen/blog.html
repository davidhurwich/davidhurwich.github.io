Tropisueño!

A fun taco spot with lots of flavor.

Entering Tropisueño feels like being recieved at an ongoing mexican party. The festive dressed servers take you to your table, which can be comunal or not, and leave you with a vast menu of options to choose. I tried having the order of tacos, which comes with two items plus rice, beans and tortillas to make the tacos yourself. My choices of tacos were beef barbacoa, carnitas and I ordered one extra one of al pastor to try it out. I found that most of their meats come in larger chunks, even the barbacoa and carnitas. The meat was pretty juicy and the tacos were a nice handul. Not too big not too small. Overrall the tacos are a nice treat, but if you go in looking for a taste of Mexico you probably won't get it there. However they do offer vegan and vegetarian options that are equally as delicious. I would consider this place a great hangout spot with your friends while in the city.
