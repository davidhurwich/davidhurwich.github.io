About Me

My name is Chris Kelly. I'm currently enrolled in the Visual Media Design department at City College of San Francisco. I've been drawing and designing for most of my life and I wanted to develop the skills necessary in order for me to introduce my artwork into a digital format. So far this semester, we have worked with applications such as Photoshop, Illustrator, Animte, and InDesign. You will be able to check out my projects that I have done with each one of these apps. It's been a fun semester and I look forward to diving deeper into these apps next semester!
