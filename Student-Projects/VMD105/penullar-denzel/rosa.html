Rosa is a TikTok character created by Adam Martinez in December 2019. The character typically addresses the camera like they are speaking to someone directly which leads to conversational TikTok duets. In TikTok videos featuring Rosa, TikTokers pretend to be teen friends who usually talk about getting into trouble or gossip, sometimes in Spanish.
