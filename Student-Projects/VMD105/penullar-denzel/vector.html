Vector is artwork made up of points, lines, and curves that are based upon mathematical equations , rather than a solid colored square pixels. This means that no matter how large or small or how close you zoom in on the image, the lines, curves, and points remain smooth. The Image I provided above is a finished project that I did using Adobe Illustrator.
