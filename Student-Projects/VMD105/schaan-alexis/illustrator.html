The purpose behind my rooster picture is actually kind of an inside joke. A long time ago my friend showed me a picture of a sign saying “chicken security force” and I thought it was funny because it could come across as the chickens being the security force.

It actually took me a long time to make both the rooster and the sign in the background. What I did was create a template of a real rooster and then use the pen tool to outline it. I also used pathfiner a lot to combine certain layers and parts.
