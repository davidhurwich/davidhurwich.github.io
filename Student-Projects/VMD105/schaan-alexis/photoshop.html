For the raster final project I was trying to go with something simple that I could apply all of my knowledge to. There are three main pictures combined. The challenge for me was making sure that the resolution of the seperate pictures looked good together. I used the lasso tool a lot for this project because I felt it had the most control. I also used the add and subtract function to make small adjustments to the selected areas.
