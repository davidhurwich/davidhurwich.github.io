Raster

The new stuff that I‘ve learned: like the selection mode, converting RGB to CMYK, etc. were all an amazing introduction to the course. This was originally to show the type AMV/retro edits that were on Youtube and the many different apps I’ve used to make these kind of edits instead of manually doing it. Overwall was a fun project to do and delve deep into to get everything sorted.
