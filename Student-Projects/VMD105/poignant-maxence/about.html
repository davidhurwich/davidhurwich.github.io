About Me

Hi! I'm Maxence, but everybody calls me Max. I'm an 18-year-old freshman from France studying graphic design in San Francisco. I'm from Lille, a city in nothern France, where I spent my whole childhood. I've always had an interest in other cultures and countries around the world! I especially like North American and Asian culture. I'm passionate about art, especially graphic design as a whole. I also like geography, history, learning languages, Asian Art History and cats.
