Animate Project

For this project my goal was to create a simple animation of my logo that could be played on repeat. the animation starts off from a simple round shape to transform into rectangular pieces that make up the logo. For the sounds, I used sounds of a piece of steel being hit. I like the result of this project but if I had to do it again I would try to make the movements smoother.
