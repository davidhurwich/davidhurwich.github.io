Shirley
VISION OF REALITY

Here is a gorgeous clip from one of my favourite film Shirley: Vision of Reality. Thirteen of Edward Hopper's paintings are brought alive by the film, telling the story of a woman, whose thoughts, emotions and contemplations let us observe an era in American history. Shirley is a woman in America in the 1930s, '40s, '50s, and early '60s. A woman who would like to influence the course of history with her professional and socio political involvement.
