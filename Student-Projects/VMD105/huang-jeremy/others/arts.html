Arts

I thought I would share a painting from one of my favourite artist Paul Wonner. He was an American artist best known for his still-life paintings done in an abstract expressionist style. The Dutch Baroque still life tradition served as a historical source for Mr. Wonner, but he typically painted objects from everyday contemporary life.
