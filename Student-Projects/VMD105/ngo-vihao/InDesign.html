_InDesign_

For my InDesign project, I continued with the idea of creepy and dark. I choose the main colors are black, gray and white, and simple shapes so that I have enough space to display pictures and text. I wanted each element to have a distinct layout position so I used shapes to divide them up so that they are clearly separated. I use black moon to create contrast, and white I arrange my works because almost all of my work is dark.
