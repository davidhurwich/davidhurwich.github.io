My first raster project was inspired by the beautiful state of Guerrero where I went to a week long folklorico dance conference to learn the dances of that region. I included the Amusga dancer with a more meztizo dancer to showcase the diversity of the region. I added texture to the background with the intricate emroidery patterns you would typically see on the "huipil" of the ladies in the region. The vield picture in the background are the faces of the 43 teachers that were kidnapped from the small town Ayotzinapa in 2014. It is a tragedy that has lingered in the minds of the residents of Guerrero and the citizens of Mexico.
