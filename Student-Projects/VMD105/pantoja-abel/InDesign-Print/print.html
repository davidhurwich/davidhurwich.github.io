My Work On InDesign

For the print and layout portion of the class we were tasked with assembling our previous projects and displaying them through the use of the InDesign program. It was quite simply fascinating and eye opening. An eye for page arrangement and everything that goes into it is of paramount importance for a D.I.Y.’er. In this project I’ve referenced the previous projects involving the Lava-Fiend, Surfer-Galactus and animated short I did on Animator.
