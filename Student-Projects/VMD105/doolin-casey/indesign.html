For my InDesign project I created a mock print brochure for one of my favorite wine regions in France, the Savoie.

I enjoyed getting to use my wine knowledge and photos that I had taken on my travels to put together a
dynamic snapshot of the region that could easy familiarize someone who is unfamiliar
with the region's history, climate, geography, grapes and cuisine.
