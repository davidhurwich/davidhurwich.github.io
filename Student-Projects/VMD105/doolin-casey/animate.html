For my time based media project I was motived by the events of the election to create an animation
that would get people motivated to vote and laugh at the same time.

I used many different vector images and had to edit the music and various sound layers to create my animation.

I enjoyed getting to have a tie in with Van Halen since Eddie Van Halen had
passed away just around the time that I was working on the animation.
