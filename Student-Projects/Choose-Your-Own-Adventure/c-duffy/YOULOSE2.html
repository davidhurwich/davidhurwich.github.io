╳ YOU LOSE! ╳

As you walk into the building you see a shadowy figure is standing in the corner of the building.

It's dark enough you can't make out who it is yet, you suddenly hear a loud yell and the figure from the corner charges at you!

His face comes into view, it is Sewage Mike! He comes down on your head with an iron pipe.

You pass away... killed by the same weapon that Mike used to kill Li'l Sebastian. You are remembered by fondly by all. Leslie also, never forgives herself for the fact that she didn't go into the building with you to find out what was going on in there.
