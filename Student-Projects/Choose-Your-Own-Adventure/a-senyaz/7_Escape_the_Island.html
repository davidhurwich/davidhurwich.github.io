Escape the Island

You followed the path for an hour through various turns and end up in a clearing. Looking closely, you see some smoke down the path. You want to investigate further.

There is a hut made up of bamboo sticks and dry leaves. There is a small fire burning, and a rabbit is being cooked. It smells like heaven. Ouch! That hurts. You turn around and see a native with his spear against down your neck. He yells something at you in his native tongue, but you don't understand anything.
