Elleknores

“Greetings,” the mermaid in the middle begins. “Welcome to Newearth. We are the Elleknores. What is your name?”

“Hello! My name is Terra,” you say.

“And where do you come from?”

“I’m from Earth.”

Some of them raise their eyebrows. Others smirk. “Ah, we figured!” a merman says.

“What do you mean?”

“Well,” the first mermaid says. “Humans are not the most attractive-looking bunch. That explains you.”

Ouch!
