Find the Snails

It starts to get a little colder, and she feels like she's getting nowhere. She starts to imagine how warm and cozy her blankets would be, all wrapped up in a big soft pile. While she daydreams, she notices a small snail with a little leaf on it.

"Hurry hurry, to Mama's!" it squeaks.

"Mama...?" Orion looks in the direction the snail is going and notices an older woman in the front yard of a house. The woman has a shawl with shimmering waves on it, while the house has the same shimmer to it. Orion squints, and realizes there are a bunch of little snails making lazy swirls on its walls.
