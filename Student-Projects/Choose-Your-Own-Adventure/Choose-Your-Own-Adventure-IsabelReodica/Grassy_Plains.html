The Grassy Plains

Terry and Orion enter the grassy plains. It's oddly quiet, as if all the insects have disappeared... A vine suddenly wraps around Orion's torso, and both her and Terry are lifted off the ground.

"I am Coriander! The plains are my territory! Anyone who trespasses gets EATEN! HA HA!" A girl emerges from between the trees. She has the features of a venus fly trap, and most likely the deadly teeth of one as well.
