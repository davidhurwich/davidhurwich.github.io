Sherlock's Hunt

You go to Scotland Yard the next day to gather more clues. Watson is still nowhere to be seen. Scotland Yard informs you that there is a murderer on the loose! A female in her 30s was murdered in the slums last Monday and they have yet to find the murderer. However, there are 2 men that that are potential suspects. You decide to investigate further...Is it Suspect A, Suspect B, or neither???
