The Emerald Pool

You again thumb through the book, searching for clues. You find a page that displays the same mushrooms which line the pool, growing as a group in a seemingly perfect circle. This is listed in the book as a sign of good luck and safe passage. You decide the only way to continue your journey is to jump in to the pool, for there is no longer a path to follow except for this very pool, which has been labled the safest path to traverse as the book signifies.
