Arrived Skyland

They arrived to Skyland. There was a huge castle in front of them. There were two guards protecting the gate.Sherlock greeted the two guards.

Sherlock: "Good afternoon gentleman. I'm Sherlock Holmes, a private detective and I'm investigating this case that the man was killed by a magical creature. I would like to meet the King of Skyland to ask a few questions."

The guards: "Follow the little dragon with red skin. He will take you guys to our king."
