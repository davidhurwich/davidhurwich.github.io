King guides the castle

Sherlock: "Thank you for your cooperation. By the way, I am interested in this castle architecture. Can you guide us around the castle? I'm sure you know everything in here."

The king: "Sure. I'm happy to guide you guys. Come follow me."

They left the huge hall with the king. While they were walking, the king told us each room.

The king: "This room is my collection of the magical stone...this room is my research room...and..." Suddenly, he stopped walking.
