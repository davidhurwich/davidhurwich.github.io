Keep asking questions

Sherlock: "I heard the rumor that you extremely hates humans and tortures them when you find them in the magical world. Is that true?"

The king: "What? You must be mistaken me for someone else. You are asking the wrong person."

Sherlock: "Really? Then why are you hiding something? You seem to finish the questioning in a rush."

The king: "HOW RUDE YOU GUYS ARE!!" PUT THEM IN JAIL!!" The king got mad and they were imprisoned forever. : There is no way to get out of jail except using the last wish.
