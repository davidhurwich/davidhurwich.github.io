Beginning

Sherlock Holmes Jr. was living in the England.  One day, Sherlock Holmes was sitting on his sofa and drinking his favorite coffee; as usual at his house. They did not have a mysterious case recently, so they were bored. Watson was looking through the newspaper to search for new incidents that Sherlock might be interested in. 

Suddenly, Watson "Sherlock! Look at this article!" He seems excited. It says,

"THE MEN WERE MURDERED BY LARGE SCAR"

"and large red feather was left in the crime scene"

Watson: "Sherlock, what do you think about this case?"

Sherlock: "Well, I guess someone killed them with a large weapon, like "

Watson: "Really? I think somebody used an animal to kill the three men because they had LARGE SCARS, Why don't we check out the crime scene since we are free this afternoon?"
