Summon a pegasus

They summoned a pegasus. They sat on his back and soon the pegasus ran away.

The pegasus took us in front of a small house surrounded by large red mushrooms with white dots. The house was subtly brighter than the sky and white spores were flying. 

Watson: "There might be somebody at home. We should knock the door to be polite to them."
