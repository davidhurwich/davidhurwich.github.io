Bump to an old lady

Old lady: "Thank you for your help. You might be the famous private detective, Sherlock Holmes Jr?"

Sherlock: "Yes, I am."

Old lady: "I was finding you. You need to solve the mystery. Otherwise, the principle of the nature will be destroyed."

Sherlock: "Tell me more about it."

Old lady: "Go to magical world. Here, I'll give you my magical stone. You can make 3 wishes on the stone. But don't forget. YOU CAN ONLY MAKE 3 WISHES. Be careful what kind of wish to make. If you get lost in the woods, try to go to this magical tree. It will guide you to solve the mystery."

Sherlock and Watson thought the old lady is saying a lie because the stone that she gave looks just a usual rock that exist in anywhere, round, gray rock. Besides, what is a magical tree?

Sherlock: "Well, thank you. I'll take it with me."

Old lady: "Have a safe journey and good luck."
