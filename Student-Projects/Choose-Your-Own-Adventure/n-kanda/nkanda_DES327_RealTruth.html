King's disclosures

The king: "Detective Sherlock, I'm sorry. I wanted to tell you the truth in that hall but I couldn't. If the guards know, I have to proclaim the abdication."

Sherlock: "Tell me the truth."

The king: "There is a rule that Skyland residents are not allowed to go down to the land. And I broke that rule. Not only this time. I've done many times."

Sherlock: "To meet his friend, right?"

The king: "You're right. My friends, not only Chunky but also other dragon friends are living on the land."

Sherlock: "So what happened that night?"

The king: "I...I...I don't know."

Nick: "What do you mean you don't know?"

The king: "I remember I went down to see my friends as usual after 10 pm and chatting with them. But...the next thing I remembered was I was in my bed the next morning. And..."
