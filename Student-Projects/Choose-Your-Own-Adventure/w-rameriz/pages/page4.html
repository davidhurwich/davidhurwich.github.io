Aliens are coming!

That was the message. Thousands of alien spaceships have been detected by the radars. They are approaching fast to the earth. Our planet might soon be under an imminent attack.

We do not know their intentions yet, but they are coming to rescue one of their members who has been captured by the government during a secret mission to the space.

We have never seen anything like this before. Everything seems that it has been taken from a film.

How would you react?
