The keypad flashes green. Sadly, it was the right answer. Everything shuts off. I can’t see anything anymore.

I hear a whirring of machinery starting up above me. I look up and I am blinded for a second. My eyes adjust and I see a projection on the floor.

There are photograms of happy moments that I spent with my daughter. I can hear her voice. I can actually see her out of this glass prison. She is here crying and shouting.

This means that by exiting this door I can finally be with her. I can finally give her the attention I needed to give to her.

I hear a click. A shaft of light illuminates the door. It’s unlocked.

What do I do now?
