Suddenly, everything lights up. I am trapped in a room with walls made of glass. On the other side of those walls, there are people staring at me.
Who are those people? I try to look for a familiar face in the crowd.
I scream for help.
Nobody cares. It seems like they are recording me with their cell phones.
I look around and I see a mysterious door on my left and a screen hung onto the wall on my right
