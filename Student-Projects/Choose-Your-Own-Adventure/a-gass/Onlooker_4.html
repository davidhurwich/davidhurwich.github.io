I am waiting.

It has probably been an hour since I have been waiting. It seems like one week.

I am used to waiting for things to happen. Apparently though, this option is not helping me to understand what is going on.

Should I do something?

I don’t think walking around the darkroom without any destination would provide me any insight.
Therefore, I should probably start counting the seconds until something happens. I have nothing to do.


One, two, three, four… ten.
