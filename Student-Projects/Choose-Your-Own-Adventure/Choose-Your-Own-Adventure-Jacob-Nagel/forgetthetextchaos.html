A peaceful life

You ignore the text and call your boys over to drink and watch Initial D however, you never get back to it. Over the days the text will eventually get buried by others and you will forget it ever existed.

Eventually a year or so later when looking through your messages on the toilet you’ll spot it and wonder what they wanted, until eventually deleting the message along with other old conversations.

Your life moves on, you have a short lived marriage, bookended by the tragic death of your wife in a bus accident. Life still goes on though, as you move through romantic partners, jobs, houses, cars, clothes, you begin to feel the passage of time, and age begins to really take a toll on your body. You die at the age of 86, you leave behind many close loved ones and family. Your life wasn’t an action movie by any means, but it was eventful and satisfying in its own unique way.
