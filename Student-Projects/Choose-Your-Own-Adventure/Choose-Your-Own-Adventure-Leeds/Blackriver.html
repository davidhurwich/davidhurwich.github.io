You head towards the riverbank. As you get closer, the water looks as if it's almost glowing. Nervous to touch it, you follow along the bank until you see a series of rocks across the river.
You start jumping from one rock to another.
Half way to the other side, you hear a low growl and then a big splash. A giant creature, as big as an alligator and as agile as a fish, jumps out of the water. Its jaws latch onto your arm.

You realize there is no escaping as it pulls you under.
