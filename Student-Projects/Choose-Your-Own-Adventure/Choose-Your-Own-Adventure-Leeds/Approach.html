Still cautious, you walk up to them. They see you and instantly stand up. Just then you knew you have made a mistake; they grab you. Still speaking in a language you don’t know, they seem excited. They tie you up and bring you to a camp.
As they bring you to a large tent, you realize they have brought you to a trade fair. Each booth is a bit different. The booth they take you to has a variety of alien species held captive in metal cages. They walk you to a cage where another human being is held captive. You look at each other in horror as they place you inside and lock the cage’s door once more.
