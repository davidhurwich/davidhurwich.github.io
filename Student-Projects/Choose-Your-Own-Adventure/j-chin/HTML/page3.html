The Temple of Wonders

A vast room with no ceiling comes into view as you enter the 20 foot doors. The sunlight shines down upon two pedestals before you. On one floats a red/orange orb, glowing bright. On the other floats a purple orb shining. These are the two magic sources Grindol has given you and now you must choose one.

The powers that come will vary and will be unique to only you.
