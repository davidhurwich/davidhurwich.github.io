Survive in the New World

Introduction

It is already 100 days into the zombie apocalypse and the government and cdc have fallen. You are alone and must learn how to survive. Pick your character, and choose wisely. What's their weapon of choice? Their main skill? You are out in the world, you need to not get killed, find food, shelter and water. Youll come across people, do you trust them? You decide.

This game is not just another hunting zombies game, try and build a community. Build a future. You will come across other survivors in the game, will you fight them? Will you befriend them? Try to survive and build a new world.
