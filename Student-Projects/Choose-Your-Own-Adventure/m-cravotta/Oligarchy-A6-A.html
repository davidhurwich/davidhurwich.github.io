You decide as a group to accept the fine for tax evasion. All seems well until you get a knock on your door from the local sheriff. He says that he needs to bring you in for questioning.

Confused and handcuffed, you sit in the cold room alone until a detective barges in. “Good afternoon Mr. Moneybags, are you an investor in The Frederick Shipment Co?” “Yes”, you respond cautiously.

“Now what would you know about a container of opium and morphine that we found earlier today coming from California, said it was going to your dock?” “I know nothing about it,” you reassure. “Well someone’s got to answer for it!” the detective quickly barks back.
