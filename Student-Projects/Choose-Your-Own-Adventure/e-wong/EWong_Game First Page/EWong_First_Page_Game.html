Hospital: Anesthesia Technologist

Anesthesia Technologist assists Anesthesiologist by setting-up equipment and preparing medications. Orders, stocks, tests, and maintains anesthesia supplies, medications, and equipment. Being an Anesthesia Technician washes and sterilizes re-usable equipment and reports malfunctioning equipment to appropriate personnel.

  • Set Up and Breakdown Anesthesia Equipment
  • Perform Equipment Testing
  • Conduct Preventative Maintenance and Sterilization
  • Facilitate Patient Care
  • Stock Anesthesia Supplies
  • Set-up for each case range of Trauma, Stroke, Craniotomy, Cardiac, Double-Lungs, Liver, etc.
  • Make different types of IVs: PA, ABP, CVP, ART, level-1, Blood pump
  • Set up Cellsaver, Belmont, Ultrasound, Glidescope, etc.
