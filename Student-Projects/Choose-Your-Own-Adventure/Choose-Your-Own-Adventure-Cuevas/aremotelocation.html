"Uh Oh, Where did it go?"

Sophie and Sansome were like peanut butter and jelly. The twins did everything together.
It was a Saturday morning, the start of the weekend, and the twins were ready to soak up their favorite cartoons like a sponge.

"Hey Sophie, where's the remote?" asked Sansome?

Sophie and Sansome looked up and down the house but could not find it.

"Did you check in the couch?" the two asked at the same time.

They looked at one another and then the couch again...
