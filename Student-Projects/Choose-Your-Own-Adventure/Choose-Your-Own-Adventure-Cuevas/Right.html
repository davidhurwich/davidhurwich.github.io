"This place gives me the creeps"

Sophie and Sansome decided to go right. Sophie touched the velvet ground, it felt just like their couch.
She’d always secretly hated that couch… and now look at the mess they were in! The whole place was made of different shades of Velvet.

“Are we inside the couch??” wondered Sansome.

Sophie and Sansome walked deeper into the plush jungle.

“I just want to find the remote and go home” whimpered Sansome. “Do you hear that?” asked Sophie. The twins could hear soft muffled chatter in the distance.
