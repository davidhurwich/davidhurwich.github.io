"We're going in!!"

Sophie and Sansome both raced to the couch, eager to find the remote before the other.
Sophie and Sansome both plunged their arms into the old purple sofa and felt around as muchas they could for the missing remote.

"I don't feel anything,"
grunted Sophie.

They both pushed their arms deeper into the cushions.

"Woah, woah wooooooah!"
yelped Sansome.

Sophie and Sansome plunged into the center of the couch. They tumbled quickly but gentle down a deep, dark tunnel and finally landed in a mysterious world.

"Where are we?"

They looked at one another and then at the sign before them...
