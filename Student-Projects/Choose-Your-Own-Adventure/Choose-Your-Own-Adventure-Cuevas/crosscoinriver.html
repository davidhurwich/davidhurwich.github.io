"Let's cross"

Sophie and Sansome cross the Coin River but before they’re on the other side they spot a trail of lint behind them.

“Has that always been there???” asked Sansome. “I don’t think so” replied Sophie.

The trail of lint began to gather at their feet. As they looked closer they noticed the lint had a face as well. They seemed to be squeaking as they gathered by their feet. After circling them a few times the trail began start down another path beside them.

“Should we follow them?” asked Sophie?
