"Crispy, Crunchy, and Crumbly"

Three, giant pieces of a chocolate-chip cookie crawled towards them from the velvet jungle. They were crumbling away as they did so, leaving a trail of crumbs behind them.

“Aaaaaaaggghhhhhh….mmmfffggghhhh” one of the crumbs grumbled. “Kiiiiiiidssss” followed another. “Don’t be afraid” sneered the last.

Sophie and Sansome looked at one another as they usually did.

“I don’t like the looks of these guys,” mumbled Sansome. “Me either,” replied Sophie.
