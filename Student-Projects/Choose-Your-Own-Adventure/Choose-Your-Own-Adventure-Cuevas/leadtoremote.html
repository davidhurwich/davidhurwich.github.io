"Let's trust the Crumbs"

The Crumbs begin to crawl back down the trail from which they appeared. The twins decided to take a chance and trust the Creepy Crumbs.

“How much further are we going to let these guys take us into the velvet jungle” Sansome asked his sister as he clung to her arm.

Sophie shrugged and hopped they were making the right decision. They just wanted to get back home and take advantage of their lazy weekend.
Finally, they reached a clearing.
