Where's the Breakfast?

You couldn’t trust a flying serpent. After all, it was the one who woke up the Oni. You decided to make a dash with the Grim Reaper into a different route. However, the Oni spotted you not too far away. The Oni felt hungry and decided to make you their breakfast.

You ran and ran, but it didn’t feel like you got too far. You asked the Grim Reaper while running, “Are you sure we’re getting anywhere??”

The Grim Reaper replied, “It should be correct, kid”.

Suddenly, you smacked into something and fell. You wondered what you crashed into, only for you to see red skin and a horned shadow. You got pale and thought, “Oh no. This is the Oni, isn’t it? Darn I should have taken the flying serpent.”

Meanwhile, the Grim Reaper tried to fight off the Oni. There was no place to escape and it dawned upon you that you were gonna be the Oni’s breakfast.

The Oni threw the Grim Reaper to the side and grabbed you by the head. You knew that you made the wrong choice and accepted your death.
