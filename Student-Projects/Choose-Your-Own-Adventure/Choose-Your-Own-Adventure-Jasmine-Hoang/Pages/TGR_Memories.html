Memories

You and the Grim Reaper continue to walk forwards in Sanguis Terra.

“So...Grim Reaper...I have a question for you. Are there like a bunch of you guiding other people too? What’s that scythe for? I thought you were supposed to harvest me with it, but I don’t think I’ve seen you do anything with it except lugging it around.”

The Grim Reaper chuckled, “Hey kiddo...calm down. Why so curious?”

“Well .... Being stuck in the hospital bed made me curious about everything. I couldn’t walk or talk before. It’s just everything that’s happening now is new to me. I guess everything is spilling out of me. I just feel so happy to be with you.”

“...That’s nice of you”, the Grim Reaper blurted. “Anyways, it’s not everyday you get to walk with a Grim Reaper in Hell to meet Hades. So enjoy this adventure while you can because you’ll probably forget all about it kid.”

You thought to yourself, “I’ll forget my only friend? My only memory being in a healthy body? What if I can’t be reincarnated?”

Suddenly, your thoughts snapped as you feel a stinging sensation on your ankle.”

“Ouch! What was that?”, as you grumbled.

The Grim Reaper turned to look and realized there was blood dripping. The Grim Reaper made a concerned face and said, “Hey kid, we have to make a run. The hellhounds are going to be after you.”

You completely forgot about the hellhounds and decide to make a dash for who knows how far wherever you needed to go was.
