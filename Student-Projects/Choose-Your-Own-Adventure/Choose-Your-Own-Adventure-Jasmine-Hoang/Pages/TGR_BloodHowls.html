Blood Howls

As you ran, you heard howling coming from every direction. You don’t know how many there are, but all you know is that there’s a lot.

“HEY KID! KEEP ON RUNNING TILL YOU PASS THAT GATE AHEAD!”

However, you were beginning to run out of energy. You never ran before, heck you couldn’t even walk. You began to slow down, panting. “I c...can’t anymore G-grim Reaper."

The Grim Reaper looks you dead in the eyes and then sees the hellhounds running closer. You noticed the Grim Reaper pulling out his scythe, and battled the hellhounds.

As time passed, you regained your energy. But at the same time, more hellhounds keep on coming and the Grim Reaper is getting exhausted.
