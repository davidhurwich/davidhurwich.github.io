Sanguis Terra

You decided to travel across dry lands. All there is are dead plants, and a few rocks here and there. From afar, you can see the mountains and lake. Otherwise, it’s practically a barren cracked land here. You muttered, “I thought this place would be crawling with all sorts of demons and creatures. It’s so dead here...”

“Well you aren’t exactly wrong”, the Grim Reaper replied. “This place is known as Sanguis Terra. If we were in the mainland…..then yeah, you would see a jackpot of demons and creatures.”

You questioned, “What’s Sanguis Terra?”

The Grim Reaper smiled and teasingly replied, “It’s better for you to not know. You would freak out like the kiddo you are.”

You give the Grim Reaper a deadpan look. “Hmph! Fine then.”

“Alright kid I’m teasing you. Sanguis Terra is latin for “Land of Blood”. Ju-”

You stuttered, “L-land of blood?? I-is that why the ground here is so red?”

“Hey kid, listen to me. As long as you don’t bleed a single drop, you’ll be fine.”

“And if I do? What happens?”, you quietly asked.

“Well then…. The hellhounds will be right at your back. Nothing is better than juicy humans like you.”

You could only shrink at the thought of hellhounds eating you.
