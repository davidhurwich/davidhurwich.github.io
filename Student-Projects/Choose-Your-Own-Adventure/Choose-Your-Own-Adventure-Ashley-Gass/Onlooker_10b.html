All my life I have been a bystander.

Finally, now, I realize the mistakes I have done throughout my life. Avoiding to take action has always been the easiest option for me. Less struggle, less hassle but I finally get it now. I see an opportunity to make things right.

I make to the door. I have nothing on my mind outside of helping my daughter and finally facing my past and the consequences I deserve related to it.
I am ready to start over.
I turn the knob. I get out.


Everything is dark. It seems like a tunnel.
