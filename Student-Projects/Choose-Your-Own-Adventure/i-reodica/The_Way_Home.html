The way home

After a few minutes, Terry and Orion make it to the swamps.

"I'm home!" Terry leaps out of Orion's arms and splashes around in his swamp. "Thanks, Dee!"

Dee nods, and as he leaves, Orion looks back on their adventure together.

"Hey, Terry, I should probably get going. It was great meeting you, though." Orion's words are bittersweet.

"It was great meeting you too, and thank you! You know the way here, Orion, you're welcome to visit any time. Promise you will?"

Orion grins. She's finally made some friends. "I promise."
