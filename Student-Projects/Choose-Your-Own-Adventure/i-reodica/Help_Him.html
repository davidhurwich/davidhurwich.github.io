Help him

"Okay, I'll help you." She picks him up, his little legs dangling, and they set out to bring Terry home. But after a couple of directional issues, the two of them start to feel hopeless.

"Is there anything that will make this easier?" Orion says.

Terry thinks for a bit and suddenly looks up at her, "Oh, I know! Maybe the Keeper of the Sails know the way! She knows where every home is."

"Why didn't you say that sooner..." Orion grumbles, but she holds on to him a little tighter, and they set out to find the Keeper. What a weird forest.
