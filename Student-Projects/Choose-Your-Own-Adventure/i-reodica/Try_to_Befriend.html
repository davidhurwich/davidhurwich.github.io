Try to befriend Coriander

"It must be lonely -rgh- being here all by yourself." Orion struggles against the vine.

"What?" Coriander growls out.

"Well, everyone who comes here gets eaten, right? You must not have anyone to talk to. But we can be friends."

"Wh- I... I guess it is kind of lonely..." Her vines get a little looser.

"We can be friends, I can visit you, and we can talk, if you just let us go so I can bring Terry home. We need to get to the swamps, do you know where it is?"

Coriander thinks it over, and is moved that someone would think of her. She lets the two of them go.

"The swamps are right past the River Path. I don't know them too well, though, so I can't help much."

"Thank you," Terry says, "We'll visit, we promise!"
