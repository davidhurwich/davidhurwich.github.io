Meet Terry

She looks down at her feet and sees a small, fat tadpole.

"Hi," he says, breathless,"M-My name is Terry! Um, if you don't mind, can you point me towards the, uh, the swamps? I just grew in my legs and got so excited to try them out, but I wandered too far. I can't tell where I am..."

It's not really her business, she doesn't even know where the swamps are, but she could try helping him out anyway.
