Bother him

"We're so close though," Orion says. She nudges the salamander awake. He's also a little slimy.

"Yaaaaaawn. Hello, who are you?"

"I'm Orion, and this is my friend Terry. We heard from the snails that you can help us find the swamps?"

"Hi Orion... Hi Terry... I'm Dee..." Dee yawns again and slowly gets up. "I can lead you to the swamps, they're really close by." He slowly pads along and looks back to the two of them with half lidded eyes. "Well? Follow me."
