July 4th, 1942, 7:15 A.M

They drive you to the port and walk with you all the way until you board your boat. The woman even bought you and Aaron a few sandwiches and a bottle of milk and a sandwich. Exhausted, you board the ship and find a space in the back where you won't be disturbed. Aaron puts his head in your lap and falls asleep. You two are a safe now.
