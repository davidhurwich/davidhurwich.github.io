WEST L.A. FADEAWAY

My story intends to represent a day in the life of John. John lives in the seedy underbelly of west L.A. He must face the challanges of the time.
	Met with rouges and refugees alike the choices center around what you do in these interactions.The goal being to find John refuge for 
	his ailing mind. If only for a miniute.
 
As he walks down the street John crosses paths with an old enemy and must decide to fight or defer. As he continues on his way John must make
	his wage. As meger as it may well be comparitive to the men who hire him. Next in his path John meets a nice girl. She seems to have 
	a keen intrst in John, and John in her. His final challenge is to find a chateau, 21 rooms, but one will do.
