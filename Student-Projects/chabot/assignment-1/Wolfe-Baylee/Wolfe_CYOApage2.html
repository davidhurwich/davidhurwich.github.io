Walk in the Park

Fancy Forest State Park

1234 Fancy Dr.

Hours of Operation: 7am-10pm Daily

Attention! Rainbow Trail is currently under renovations.

Things to do:

  • Take a dip at Fancy Creek Waterfall. (No lifeguard on duty)
  • Ride by Fancy Creek on horseback!
  • Bird watch! Identify all 143 bird species living in the trees.

Don’t forget to stay on trail and wear sunscreen!

Home to all kinds of plants and Wildlife:

  • Vulpes vulpes
  • Vespa mandarinia
  • Toxicodendron radicans
Please respect the natural habitats!

Calendar:

  • 06/03 at sundown - Cal’s Night Walk Spot all the creatures that spook the night.
