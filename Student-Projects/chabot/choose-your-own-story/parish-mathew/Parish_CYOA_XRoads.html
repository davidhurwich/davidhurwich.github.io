The Crossroads

The guards wish you safe travels as you leave the Fort. Barely a five minute walk from the fort you make it to a crossroads. A signpost is embedded in the grass with multiple languages telling you the directions. To the north it says “Farm Road” with a piece of parchment nailed to that sign saying “DANGER.” Pointing east is a sign saying “To Serde.” Someone carved a holy symbol into it. Pointing to the south is a sign that says “Trade Road.” It doesn't look modified.
