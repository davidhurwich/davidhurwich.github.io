You pay the toll

You drop ten gold coins into the rocky hands of the troll. It gives you a warm, stony smile and offers you a meal in the building. You politely decline, not feeling hungry and eager to get to the city. You pass by the troll and continue on your journey.

As you walk along the cobbled road you start hearing the sound of metal clanging against metal. Coming up on the road you see a group of dwarves battling with a mechanical construct. A clockwork soldier. The dwarves appear to be winning. You can try to lend a hand, run around the conflict, or wait and watch.
