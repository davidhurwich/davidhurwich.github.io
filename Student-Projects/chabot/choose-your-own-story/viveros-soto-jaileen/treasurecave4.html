Treasure Cave - Adventure In Nature

They walked for hours exploring and looking at beautiful plants and animals. When they least expected, they were walking in the middle of nowhere. There were tall green trees with big round pine cones growing out of them, flowers of all different shapes and colors, beautiful birds singing while the sun was brightly shining.

The silence was peacefully relaxing to the mind, it allowed them to think clearly. They became instantaneously curious to explore their surroundings when they came across a cave.
