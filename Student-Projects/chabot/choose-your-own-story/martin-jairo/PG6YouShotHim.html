City Nights

After picking up the gun you yell at him "Do not move one inch! I will shoot!" The criminal calls your bluff, but you were not bluffing. You shoot him dead and the bullet echos throughout the sleepless city. You drop the gun as you hear people panic around you as your adrenaline starts to drop. Cops show up to the scene. What do you do?
