Scene 1B: Home Sweet Home

You finally arrive home and get your nighttime routine started. First, you check for any new emails and text messages from your colleagues and friends. Second, scrub-a-dub-dub away that built-up sweat while in the shower. Third, catch up on the latest news. And lastly, enjoy some last-minute Netflix bingeing before getting drowsy.

Do you want to sleep now? You have to get ready for work in about six hours. You should be cautious with how you spend your time nowadays.
