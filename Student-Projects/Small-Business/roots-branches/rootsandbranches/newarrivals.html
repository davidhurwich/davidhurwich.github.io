Zebra Plant $60

The Aphelandra Squarrosa, or Zebra Plant, has spectacular foliage and exotic-looking flowers. Zebra plants require very bright, but indirect light. Do not place in direct sun, especially if you want them to bloom. Keep the soil moist, but not soggy, and never allow the soil to totally dry out. Bottom leaves drop off a Zebra plant when it is over or under-watered.
