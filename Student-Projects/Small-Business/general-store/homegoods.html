Malka Dina

We've admired Malka Dina's minimal chic ceramics before, so it's no surprise that the latest collection from the artist, designer, and New York native behind the Brooklyn ceramics brand, Elana Noy is so next-level beautiful. With shapes inspired by nature, the body, and ancient architecture, plus so much textural variety — from ridged arches to organic, bubbled glazing — we could find a place to house each vase, mug, bookend, and incense holder.


"Running my own business and getting to do what I love every day has made this the most creatively fulfilling and challenging time of my life."

Elana Noy
