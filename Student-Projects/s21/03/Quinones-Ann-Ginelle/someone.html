Look for Someone Else

As you head down the hallway, your hunger and rage is growing and growing by the second. It's as if you're in a blind rage, all you see is red. You start to panic and run faster. Next thing you know, you bump into someone so hard, you crash onto the floor.

You look up and see a tall man with wavy brunette hair brushed off to the side. You noticed he's also pretty lean to have you flying to the ground from running into him. What happened? He's also in a suit, as if he's dressed up for the wedding, but you don't recognize him. Who is he?

"There you are. And where do you think you're running off to?" he says.

As you scramble up on your feet, you beg him for help. "Please sir, can you help me? I don't know what's going on."

"You're in the process of becoming a vampire, and I can help you. Let's go for a walk," he says as he hold out his hand like a gentleman.

A what?!? A VAMPIRE!? He must be crazy, right?

What do you do?
