Follow Kenneth

I mean...maybe he's crazy, but you can't explain what's happening anyway. So you tell him to start explaining.

"I'm sorry I had to do this to you, but I mixed my blood in your glass of wine and killed you. To fully transition to become a vampire, you have to feed", he explains.

"Wait...you killed me?! And, what do you mean feed?" You question as you start to panic.

"Calm down, Love. Don't worry about all that. Your emotions are heightened right now so you need to stay calm. And, you're going to have to feed on human blood or else you'll return to being a corpse and die, permanently. And, I can help you", he says.

"Who... are you? And how do you know so much?"

"My name is Kenneth, and I'm also a vampire."
