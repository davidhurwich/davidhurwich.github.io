Suddenly, you feel a tightness in your chest and you can't breathe. You look down and see an arm reaching through your chest and a hand clutching your heart. Your body starts to feel cold starting from your finger tips and toes, working it's way up to your chest, where your heart was.

"What a shame", says Kenneth.

Everything goes black his arm pulls back out of your chest, with your heart still in his hand, as you collapse on the ground.
