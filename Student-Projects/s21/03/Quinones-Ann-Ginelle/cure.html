"Is there a cure?"

You were partially joking when you asked but, all of a sudden, Kenneth's face looked...disappointed.

"If there was, would you take it? Do you really want to be human again? Even after everything I have shown you?" he said staring into the distance.

"This past month has been great but, I just don't feel like myself anymore. I feel like I'm living a lie or like I'm living someone else's life, not mine. I recognize myself or my life and..."

Just then, Kenneth pushes you down to lay flat on the ground as he kisses you deeply and passionately. Even though it was a shock, you felt a connection. Once he slowly seperated, he whispers, "I love you."

Just then, as you're gazing into his eyes, you feel a pinch on your shoulder. You look and see he stabbed you with a syringe. What did he do?
