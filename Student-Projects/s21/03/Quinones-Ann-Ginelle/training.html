Training with Kenneth

"First, drink this", says Kenneth as he tosses you a blood bag.

Without even thinking, you rip open the bag and suck it dry like a juicebox. "That's it? Do you have more? I need more."

"Which leads us to lesson #2. You can learn how to feed without killing people or I can teach you how to control your agility to feed on animals", says Kenneth.

What's this? A vampire with empathy? I guess it's worth learning from him.
