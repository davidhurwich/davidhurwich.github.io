The Park

You eat, get dressed, and head out. The three ghost siblings follow you as you head out the door.

As you arrive at the park, Rin and Ben already went somewhere else on their own. You notice people that are floating around and faded just like the three siblings.

“Oh them? They are ghosts too but they can’t detach themselves from that area. They must hold some regrets after they died,” Ken explains.

You look at the three siblings and wonder…

“What are you even doing here anyways?”, Ken asks.

“To take a jog”, you reply. But by the time you reply, Ken has already headed somewhere else too.

On your left, you hear a kid crying and on your right, you hear two seniors having a heated argument. For both the kid and senior, you see a ghost covered in darkness hovering over them. You should see what’s wrong.

Who do you approach?
