Living Room

You leave your room and slowly head to the living room. You see a moving figure in the dark.

You turn on the lights but realize nothing is there until...

You turn around to see three kids in front of you. They are strangely faded in appearance…

The girl starts talking to the two other boys, “Hey do you think she can see us?”

Boy: “No way, no normal human can see us... right?”

Another boy stares at you while you stare back at him. You look down to see him sticking out his hand as if he was asking for a handshake.

“Hey, hey, hey, she can see us!”, he shouts in a panicked manner while shoving the other two.

The girl looks straight at you and glares. “Hey, you. You can see us right?”, she asks.
