The Next Day

You see more malicious ghosts than usual roaming around the city and they seem more dangerous than the ones you usually encounter.

You call Tina and tell her about this. She arrives at your location and looks around.

“Where are these coming from?”

“This seems to be the area where they are gathering around and we don’t know why”, Ken says.

“Danger”, Ben randomly says.

A familiar cloud of smoke appears before you. Rin, Ken, and Ben back away while Tina quickly stands between you and the cloud of smoke with a serious expression.

“Dust…”, Tina mumbles.

“Well, hello again little friend”, Dust says happily.

“Is this your doing?”, Tina asks.

“Oh, if it isn’t one of the SouLs? How do you like my minions?”

“Hope you like my present. I have something to do so I’ll see you around, new friend.” The smoke then dissipates.

The group of malicious ghosts start attacking. Thirty minutes had passed and Tina was struggling to stand her ground.

“What do we do?”, Rin says as the three siblings panic.

You notice one of the malicious ghosts was going to attack Tina.
