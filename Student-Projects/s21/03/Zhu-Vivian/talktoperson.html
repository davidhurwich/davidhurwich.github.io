Way to the Grocery Store

You walk up to the person and ask if they were okay. They reply and tell you that they were just having a bad day. Nothing seemed to be going their way. They thank you for asking if they were okay.

The ghost that was hovering over them disappears. You split ways and head back to the three kids.

“Hey, that was dangerous! Did you know that the ghost could hurt you?” Rin shouts.

“What happened to the ghost?”, you ask.

“The person’s negative feelings must have disappeared, so the ghost disappeared”, she says.

As you are about to continue on your way to the grocery store, a girl approaches you.

“Hey, I saw what you did. Good job.”, the girl says while smiling.

“My name is Tina. I know you are confused but I assume these three here are your friends?”

You nod hesitantly.

A red light glows from her hand. “This is my power. I use this to cleanse those malicious ghosts.”

“Let me cut straight to the point. I am a part of a group that helps cleanse those ghosts that you saw and I see that you have potential.” She hands over a business card that reads, SouL.

“Will you join us?”
