Unknown Room

Dust disappears with Rin, Ken, and Ben.

Backup arrives and brings Tina to their hideout to treat her. Tired from the fight, you black out.

...

You wake up in an unfamiliar room.

“You awake?”

You look to your right to see Tina looking at you.

“We both passed out at the end and Dust escaped”, she says.

“What happened to Rin, Ken, and Ben?”, you ask.

She doesn’t answer your question. “We will get them back”, she replies with a sad smile.

You look down and think about Rin, Ken, and Ben.

Where have they gone?

The same tall man from before walks into the room.

“Are you guys ready?”, he asks.

“Ready for what?”, you ask confused.

“To train, what else?”, the man smiles.
