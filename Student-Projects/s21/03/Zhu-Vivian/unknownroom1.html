Unknown Room

A bright blue light emits from your hand and you deal the final blow to Dust. He disappears along with Rin, Ken, and Ben.

The surrounding malicious ghosts that were controlled by Dust also start to disappear. Backup arrives and brings Tina to their hideout to treat her. The same tall man from before walks up to you and pats you on the back: “You did good.”

He then heads back to talk with his fellow associates.

You look at the leftover debris from the battle.

Did I make the right choice? Was it finally over?

Tired from the fight, you black out.

...

You wake up in an unfamiliar room.

“You awake?”

You look to your right to see Tina looking at you.

“I heard you defeated Dust. You made the right choice. I’m sure Rin, Ken, and Ben would have wanted this. They have been here too long.”

You don't say a word but instead, you look out the window and wonder…
