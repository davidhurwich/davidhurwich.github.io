Bedroom

“I can see you… ”, you answer.

The three kids look at each other and their scary glares turn into a smile. They gather around you and cheer: “This is the first time we have met a normal human being who could see us!”

“Let us introduce ourselves. My name is Rin, these two are my younger brothers.”

“Hello, my name is Ken.”

“Hiii, I’m Ben”, the youngest shouts happily.

“As you can see, we are ghosts,” Rin says.

Ghosts…? This was the last thing you remembered before you blacked out.
