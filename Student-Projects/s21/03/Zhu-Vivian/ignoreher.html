Bedroom

You head back to your room and shut the door.

You shut down your computer and get into bed.

I must be hallucinating… I need some sleep.

The next thing you know is you hear the door opening, and the three kids appear right in front of your face.

“Hey! My name is Rin. These two are my younger brothers. The older one is Ken and the younger one is Ben. We're ghosts.”

Ghosts…? This was the last thing you remembered before you blacked out.
