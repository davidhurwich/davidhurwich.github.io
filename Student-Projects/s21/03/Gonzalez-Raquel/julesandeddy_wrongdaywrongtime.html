Jules and Eddy: Wrong Day, Wrong Time

Jules and Eddy are in San Francisco, CA going Downtown for an interview as software engineers. When they get to the building, they are told to go to the eightieth floor. As they are chatting away, they do not see the sign on the elevator that says "Do Not Enter". The elevator starts acting up and so scared and frightened, they start hitting all the buttons. Finally, the elevator stops but when the doors open, they are in an unknown room.

Will you help Jules and Eddy?
