The Chosen One

The Council of Monsters has chosen you as the one Earthling to embark on a journey through the Mystical Monsterland! Your mission is to safely find the Camp of Friendly Floofs before the Wicked Whatchamacallits catch you. You will meet monsters of all kinds, but don't worry, there will be monsters along the way to help you out, just watch out for Spike, the sneaky monster who loves playing tricks on anyone who crosses its path. Choose who to trust wisely, Earthling. Do you accept this mission?
