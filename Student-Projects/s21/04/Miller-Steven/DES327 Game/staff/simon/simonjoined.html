You decide to approach the assassin and introduce yourself. Without looking you directly in the eye, he does the same.

“I’m Simon, I’m a ninja from the Inner Circle, although I guess these days I would consider myself more freelance. I’m surprised you didn’t attack me, not everyone out here in the woods is so kind. What brings you to these parts?”

You explain to him that you are on a journey.

“A journey? That’s not very specific… but I guess it’s more specific than wandering around the woods. Mind if I join you?”

Simon has joined your party!
