You know what, maybe he’s a gentle giant! You decide to walk up to him and introduce yourself, he could be a great ally after all… he turns and returns introductions.

“My name is Todd, I’m the stalwart paladin of the knights of nine. I’m out here to protect helpless travelers passing through these woods. Do you happen to be a helpless traveler?”

You think for a second, and then nod. For what it’s worth, you haven’t been in a single fight since you got your weapon, so you may as well have some back up for when you do.

“Alright then! I will accompany you on your travels and be your shield!”

Todd has joined your party!
