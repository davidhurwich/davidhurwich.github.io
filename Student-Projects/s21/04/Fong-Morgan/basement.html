Something tells you to trust the note, as you believe it is a cry for help. Luckily, you brought matches and gasoline with you in case you had to burn anything. You find the basement and after going through a few rooms you are surprised to discover dozens of mutilated corpses preserved in formaldehyde tanks.

You start to piece together that this place is rampant with ghosts because their tortured bodies and souls were never laid to rest after all these centuries.

Will you burn the corpses as the note requests or leave while you can?
