Finding Skippy

Go To The Streetfood Vendor

You walk over to the streetfood vendor and hope Skippy is there. But just your luck he is not!

You talk to the owner: "Hey has skippy been over here? I lost him earlier today and have been looking for him all over the place."

He answers: "Yes I just saw skippy about 30 minutes ago he just finished some puppy icecream and went across the street. I thought it was weird that you weren't with him."

You are getting closer to finding skippy. You think to yourself, "well if he isn't here then he must be at the pet store."

Go to the pet store.
