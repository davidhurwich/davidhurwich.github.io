ABOUT US

OUR STORY

When Jill, Julia, and Katie decided to start Ocean Beach Surf Shop in 2020, they wanted to create a core shop that catered to all levels of the San Francisco surf community. For the last 2 years they were proud to cater to the south end of the beach (and beyond) and provide all types of gear to the local denizens of the sea. OB hosted countless events: foggy back-lot barbecues, movie nights, surf swaps, art shows, benefits, skate jams and board demos - and loved every one. They are happy with their new home on Judah Street in the Outer Sunset.

OB Surf Shop continues to carry some of the most trusted brands in the business, and we are always looking for and encouraging quality new shapers and brands. If you are looking for some new apparel, a new suit, board, or accessory, our staff will use their combined knowledge to accommodate you and your budget.

We pride ourselves on our no-pressure sales staff, and want our customers to get the best gear for their needs - whether or not we make the sale. We want surfers, old and new, to find the gear and boards that make them wanna get out in the water everyday.


With Love, the Fruit Ladies.
