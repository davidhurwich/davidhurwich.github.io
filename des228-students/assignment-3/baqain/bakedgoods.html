If you are interested in placing an order of more than ½ a dozen items or want to be certain to have specific items which may be sold out, then placing a “special order” may be a good option for you! We do not offer “holds” in the bakeshop. Retail items are first come first serve, unless a special order is placed by following the guidelines below:

Please note, our shop is busy and we can’t always answer the phone. We reccomend placing an order on Mondays and Tuesdays during 8-1PM, which is when our bake staff are preparing the baked goods.

All orders should be placed through phone call (650-756-9360), and be reflective of our inventory, with the items and quantities that you would like. Don’t forget that we do only sell certain items by the 1/2 dozen; this information is in the product description section.

Orders must be placed and confirmed at least 3 days/72 hours in advance of the pick-up date.

It is required for us to take down contact information, including a holding credit card, orders are only complete when all information have been confirmed. You will get a text message confirming your order.

Orders are only available for pick-up Wednesday through Sunday as the shop is closed Mondays and Tuesdays.
