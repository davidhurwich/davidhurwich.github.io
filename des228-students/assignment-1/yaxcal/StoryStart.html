Impacting Your Story-Start

You decide to start your journey. You get up off the dirt path that you were lying on, dusting yourself off and looking around you. Looking around, you see a dirt path towards your left, going far into the trees. Looking ahead of you, there is a path that leads higher up. Maybe you can get a better view up there? On your right, you can hear in the distance the sound of water falling down a stream, perhaps a waterfall. Which one will you decide?

Where do you go from here?

Forward up the higher path?
Towards the dirt path on the left?
Or to your right, to the waterfall?

Only you get to decide.
