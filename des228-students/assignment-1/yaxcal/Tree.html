Impacting Your Story-Tree

Finally, you have reached the large tree, which up close is so bright that it nearly blinds your eyes. You take notice of the trunk and have it spiral into a face with eyes glowing light blue. As you get closer to the tree, you hear it again.

Unlucky or lucky? Which one are you?

The tree was speaking to you in some way.

"Choose a course which will lead to your future. Choose which door you wish to enter."

Looking around, you see two doors with a lantren on your left side and right side. They look the same, only the left side is filled with more trees surrounding it.

Which one will you choose?

The left door or the right door?
